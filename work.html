BrightSide Produce

Website Redesign UX Case Study

BrightSide Produce Overview

BrightSide Produce is a Twin Cities nonprofit organization that strives to reduce neighborhood food inequities by providing fresh produce through a home subscription delivery service and as a produce distributor for corner stores in underserved neighborhoods. We redesigned their website for a boot camp team project. BrightSide loved our direction and plans to continue working with us toward a published live website.

THE PROBLEM

Usability testing found that a clear value proposition and organization mission are hard to find and that large, unbroken blocks of body text, poor navigation icon visibility, a lack of information hierarchy and inconsistent image usage generally made the site difficult to navigate.


THE SOLUTION

  • Create a responsive navigation and UI design that looks great at all breakpoints
  • Improve donation and product-purchase user flows
